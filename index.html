AUTO BIOGRAPHY

Jesus Ortiz Paz is a Mexican singer, rapper, and musician. He is well known as the frontman and lead singer of the famous Mexican regional band Fuerza Regida, whose music has taken Mexico by storm and is also making a big impact on the American charts. The band consists of lead vocalist Jesús Ortíz Paz, backing vocalist and twelve-string guitarist Samuel Jaimez, rhythm guitarist Khrystian Ramos, sousaphone player José "Pelón" García and tololoche player Moisés López.
